Discover the key benefits

Engineered with hybrid LLM reasoning and unified memory to drive fast, autonomous, and accountable customer resolutions at scale.

Faster, more accurate resolution

A hybrid architecture pairs SESTEK's proprietary NLU with large language model reasoning. Structured requests are handled with the precision and speed of deterministic workflows, while the LLM takes over for open-ended, dynamic conversations.

Autonomous, accountable decision-making

AI agents make self-executed, data-driven decisions to resolve customer requests without human intervention, and every decision is visible to Conversation Intelligence, so performance is measured, not assumed.

Hyper-personalization at scale

Short-term and long-term memory let AI agents retain context across a conversation and across a customer relationship, extracting patterns and adapting responses without asking customers to repeat themselves.
